Safety Resources & Tips

ü§ù Dealing with Bullying

  • Tell someone: Talk to a teacher, counselor, or trusted adult
  • Don't fight back: This usually makes things worse
  • Stay with friends: Bullies usually target people who are alone
  • Be confident: Stand tall, make eye contact, and speak clearly
  • Keep records: Write down what happened and when

üíª Online Safety

  • Never share personal info: Keep your address, phone number, and school private
  • Use strong passwords: Make them long and unique for each account
  • Think before posting: Once it's online, it's there forever
  • Report cyberbullying: Use platform reporting tools and tell adults
  • Be kind online: Treat others how you want to be treated
